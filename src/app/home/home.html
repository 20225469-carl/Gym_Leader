Welcome to the Pokémon Regional Hub

Explore the history and lineages of the foundational Pokémon League regions.

The Kanto Region

Kanto is known as the birthplace of modern league competitions. Characterized by urban engineering and industrial hubs like Saffron and Celadon Cities, Kanto features straightforward, raw-power combat styles. Its geography relies heavily on interconnected roads, rural towns, and volcanic islands.

The Johto Region

Johto is steeped in folklore, mythology, and deep traditions. Situated right west of Kanto, Johto embraces its cultural roots through historical architecture such as the Bell Tower and Sprout Tower. Trainers here emphasize strategic typing synergies and respect the ancient ties between humans and Pokémon.
